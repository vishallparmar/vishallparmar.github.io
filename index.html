About Me .
  • Age : 28
  • Residence: Halifax, CA
  • Hometown: Mumbai, IN
Hello World! I'm a 28-year-old model and a software engineer based in Halifax, Nova Scotia, a beautiful place in east coast of Canada that provides all I need without the craziness of a big city and I hail from Mumbai, a city that never sleeps, dares you to dream, and is the craziest city in India <3.

I bring a unique blend of creativity and technical know-how to the table because I have a deep enthusiasm for both modelling and software engineering. I'm eager to build a name for myself in the modelling business, including runway, print, and commercial modelling. I'm committed to always honing my craft, staying current with style, and working with experts in the field to produce fascinating visual experiences.

Before getting into modeling, I had a brief career as a software engineer, so with the help of the experience in that genre did I create this website in order to interact with the wider world while being as transparent as I can be and yet keeping a lot of myself to myself.

I appreciate you taking the time to get to know me. I am looking forward to engaging with possible collaborators, agencies, brands, etc. in order to work together to create something fantastic for their respective labels.

Bella ciao! :)
